City Of Joy

Kolkata (formerly Calcutta), city, capital of West Bengal state, and former capital (1772–1911) of British India. It is one of India’s largest cities and one of its major ports. The city is centered on the east bank of the Hugli RiverKolkata was the capital of the British India until 1911, when the capital was relocated to Delhi.
